Our vision

A just, compassionate and vibrant community.

Our mission

We will advance efforts to eliminate the root causes and effects of poverty and discrimination by:

  • Seeking systemic and sustainable solutions to historically intractable problems
  • Investing in nonprofit organizations committed to building their capacity to serve community needs
  • Pursuing opportunities for collaborative philanthropic effort

Our work

We are proud to support and partner with nonprofit organizations, foundations, and cross-sector coalitions in Greenville and Upstate South Carolina that share our aspirations for our community.
